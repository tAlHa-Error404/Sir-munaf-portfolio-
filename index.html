Journey of Muhammad Munaf Majeed

I'm Mohammad Munaf Majeed—a cybersecurity strategist, technologist, and mentor with a legacy spanning over 30 years in Pakistan's IT industry. My journey began in 1989 and took a defining turn in 1998 when I successfully mitigated one of the country's first major cyberattacks, setting the tone for a career committed to national digital defense.

As the Founder and CEO of Server4Sale, I've grown a reputation for delivering enterprise-grade cybersecurity with local agility—setting a benchmark for 30-minute resolution times in critical response scenarios.

Public Roles:

  • • CEC Member, P@SHA for IT & ITES (PASHA)
  • • Chair, Cyber Security and Cloud committees at PASHA
  • • Chair, CyberCrime & Security at FPCCI
  • • Co-Chair, Industry Engagement at PASHA

Beyond leadership, I dedicate time to mentoring human resource, startups, guiding professionals, and advising institutions, believing that knowledge shared is resilience built.
